# Kraken Koffee – 2023 Half-Time Sales Review Dashboard
A comprehensive Power BI dashboard analyzing sales performance across Kraken Koffee’s new **Florida Sales Region** and its three new store locations.

## Project Overview
This dashboard highlights key sales metrics, product performance, customer behavior patterns, and a full-year sales forecast based on the current 2023 trajectory. It provides insights into:

1. **Performance across Miami, Orlando, and Tampa**
2. **Top-selling product categories and items**
3. **Customer purchasing behavior across hours and days**
4. **Annual sales projections for each location**

## Key Business Insights

### Overall Sales Performance
- By mid-2023, total sales reached **$698K** across **149K** transactions.  
- Average Order Value (AOV): **$4.69**  
- Best-selling item by quantity: **Crow’s Nest Croissant**  
- Top revenue generator: **Davy Jones’ Sustainably Grown Organic Hot Chocolate (Lg)**  

### Product Drivers
- Major category contributions: **Coffee (38.6%)**, **Tea (28.1%)**, **Bakery (11.8%)**  
- Most high-performing items belong to coffee and tea product groups.

### Customer Behavior Patterns
- Peak purchasing time: **10:00 AM**  
- Monday has the highest revenue; Saturday the lowest.  
- Sales before 7 AM and after 7 PM represent only **3.6%** of total revenue.  
- Management is evaluating **changes in operating hours** to match demand patterns.

### 2023 Forecast
- Expected year-end sales: **$1.409M**  
- Ambitious stretch goal: **$1.59M**  
- Strong growth April–June suggests the target may be within reach.
